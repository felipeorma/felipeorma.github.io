About Me

I am a football data analyst with experience in scouting, big data, and creating tools to analyze player performance. I have a strong background in data science and a passion for football. I focus on creating and implementing personalized processes for talent identification and evaluation. My methodology combines traditional scouting based on direct observation and video analysis, with advanced data analysis tools. I have worked with various sources of information, both internal and external, improving player performance assessment and decision-making precision. One of my main interests is data visualization and the use of advanced metrics to perform detailed analyses at both the team and individual player levels. I am always looking for ways to integrate statistical analysis with player evaluation to offer complete solutions in scouting and performance improvement.
